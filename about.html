About

The main theme for this dataset will be based on the music of Hans Zimmer. The music pieces are piano based and i have selected two score. One being from the movie Interstellar called Day One. And the other from the movie Man of Steel called What Are You Going To Do When You Are Not Saving The World. Hans Zimmer is a popular composer who has over 100 credits to his name and I have been a fan of the majority of his work, I wanted to focus on two pieces of music.
